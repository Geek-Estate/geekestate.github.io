Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What is GEM Crystal?

A the paid newsletter offering a curated selection of industry developments and outside the box thinking, thought-provoking long-form essays, and a quarterly earnings radar to keep tabs on late stage players operating in the public markets.

Q. Who should read?

Founders, executives, practitioners, and even proptech enthusiasts. In short, anyone with an intellectual curiosity for where real estate and the Built World intersect with technology.

Q. What is included in the GEM Crystal offering?

Weekly Radars (4x per month): A curated digest of real estate, startups, and built world links & analysis blended with out-of-the-box ideas.

Weekly Transmissions (2x per month): Thought-provoking long-form articles covering a wide spectrum of topics, from innovative shipping container co-living spaces to the battle for listing acquisition in the first iBuyer world war.

Quarterly Earnings Radar (1x per quarter): A synthesis of the most relevant topline information from the sector's financial reports and balance sheets.

Q. How much does it cost?

GEM Crystal is available at a subscription price of $60/quarter or $200/year. If you sign up for the annual subscription, you get over 15% off.

Q. What is the cancellation policy?

You can cancel anytime. If you find that GEM Crystal does not meet your expectations within seven days of subscribing, please reach out to our support team -- we'll be happy to refund you and close out your subscription.

Q. Can I contribute or submit content to the newsletter?
