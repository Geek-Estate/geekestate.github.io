Membership Criteria

Adhere to GEM's Core Member Values:

  • Be Present and Engaged
  • Support the Community
  • Show Curiosity
  • Harness Authenticity
  • Do The Work

Fit one or more of the following:

  • Founders of proptech companies with a product in the market.
  • A minimum of five years of operational experience in the proptech industry, and hold a C-level, owner, or investor role.
  • C-level roles can include
    • President
    • CEO
    • Chairperson of the Board
    • Managing Director
    • Managing Partner
    • Chief Product Officer
    • Chief Marketing Officer
    • Chief Operating Officer
    • Chief Revenue Officer
    • or Equivalent title
  • VP or above at a public company.
  • If a consultant or in professional services, must be referred by at least two active GEM members who have worked with you directly.
  • Tech-forward industry professional with ten years experience.
